v aliant
a novel by Sahana Thirumazhusai

VALIANT is a loose adaptation of both Cinderella and Robin Hood, and I really do mean loose. There are no naive pining girls, one-dimensional men whose best quality is their looks, or evil ugly women who are villains because they are evil ugly women.

There is also a lack of suave foxes, but I'm willing to work that in for the right mentor.

You might be interested in VALIANT if you want a YA Fantasy novel featuring:

- A princess with personality, ambition, and flaws

- A lady-in-waiting who gets to be the main character, instead of the sidekick

- Characters inspired by Indian mythological heroes, like Rama and Lakshmana, and the Pandava brothers

- Masked vigilantes, heists, and terrorist groups

- A world based on ancient India where nature holds the magic, not people

As an Indian-American who grew up reading fairy tales and Indian mythology side by side, I wrote VALIANT to be the type of representation I wanted as a child.
